# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Figure It Out ("Figure It Out," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and discloses information when you access or use the Figure It Out mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require account registration, and we do not knowingly collect your name, email address, phone number, or contact list.

The app may collect or process the following information through third-party services:

- Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data through Google AdMob
- App analytics data and custom gameplay events through Firebase Analytics
- Crash reports, error logs, stack traces, and related diagnostics through Firebase Crashlytics
- Device information such as device model, operating system version, and app performance data
- Anonymous usage information related to ads and app interactions

The app also stores game progress locally on your device, including:

- Current level
- Placed pieces
- Hint count
- App settings such as sound, vibration, and dark mode

This locally stored data remains on your device unless you remove the app or clear its storage.

2. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

- To operate and improve the app
- To display advertisements
- To save game progress and user settings
- To diagnose crashes, errors, and performance issues

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information in accordance with their own terms and privacy policies, including:

- Google AdMob
- Firebase Analytics
- Firebase Crashlytics
- Google Play services
- Apple platform services, if applicable

You should review the privacy policies of those services for additional information.

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may disclose information only to service providers that assist in operating the app, delivering ads, or supporting analytics and crash reporting.

5. Children

Figure It Out is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Retention of Information

Locally stored game data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Third-party services may retain information in accordance with their own retention policies.

7. Your Choices

You may:

- Disable ads where supported by device settings or ad preferences
- Clear app storage to remove saved game progress and settings
- Uninstall the app to remove local data from your device
